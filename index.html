ABOUT

Dry ice is the solid form of carbon dioxide. It is used primarily as a cooling agent, but is also used in fog machines in theaters for dramatic effect. Its advantages include a lower temperature than ice water and leaving no residue (other than frost from moisture in the atmosphere). Useful for preserving frozen food where mechanical cooling is not available.
